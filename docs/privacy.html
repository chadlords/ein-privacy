Privacy Policy

Early Intervention Notes iOS App

Last updated: June 10, 2026

Introduction Information We Collect How Your Data is Handled Children's Privacy Sharing by You Security & Retention Your Rights

1. Introduction

Early Intervention Notes (“we”, “our”, or “us”) provides a free iOS application that helps licensed early intervention professionals document therapy sessions. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the app.

Important: Early Intervention Notes is a client-side tool. All data you enter is stored exclusively in your personal iCloud account and on your device. As the developer, I do not have access to, collect, store, or view any of the information you enter into the app.

2. Information Stored in the App

The app allows you to enter and store the following types of information locally on your device and in your private iCloud account:

  • Provider profile details (name, service type, Local ID)
  • Service Coordinator names, phone numbers, email addresses
  • Parent/guardian contact information
  • Child information (name, date of birth, Local ID, goals)
  • Session documentation, including handwritten notes created with Apple Pencil, targets, outcomes, and signatures

None of this information is sent to me or any third-party servers I control. It remains entirely under your control.

3. How Your Data is Handled

The app functions like a digital notebook or spreadsheet (similar to Excel or Apple Notes).

  • All data stays in your private iCloud account and on your device.
  • I (the developer) never receive, view, or have access to any data you enter.
  • The app does not send your data to any servers I control.
  • You control everything — including when and how you export or share information.

The only actions the app performs with your data are the ones you initiate yourself (such as generating & emailing a PDF or creating & emailing a ZIP export).

4. Children's Privacy

The app is intended for use by licensed professionals to document services provided to children ages 0–3. All information about children is entered and managed solely by the professional using the app. I do not collect or have access to any child data.

Parents or guardians who wish to access or request deletion of information about their child should contact the professional who is using the app.

5. Sharing and Exports (Controlled by You)

Any sharing of information happens only when you choose to do so. The app provides tools for you to:

  • Generate and email PDF session notes to parents/guardians, service coordinators, or managers
  • Create password-protected ZIP exports for monthly reporting

These exports are initiated and controlled entirely by you, just as you would if you were using a spreadsheet.

6. Data Security and Retention

Your data is protected by Apple’s built-in device encryption and your personal iCloud security settings. Because the data never leaves your iCloud account (unless you export it), security is managed by Apple and your own device settings.

You are in full control of how long data is retained. You can delete individual sessions, children, or the entire dataset at any time directly in the app.

7. Your Rights and Choices

Because all data remains under your control, you can:

  • View, edit, or delete any information directly in the app at any time
  • Export your data whenever you want
  • Turn iCloud sync on or off through your device settings
  • Delete the app and all associated data from your device and iCloud

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be reflected by updating the date at the top of this page.
